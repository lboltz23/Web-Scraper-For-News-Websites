
      Mpox cases in the US are on the rise as vaccination rates lag and new threats loom
    

The United States has seen an increase in mpox cases this year compared to last year, leading to concerns about transmission risks.

Experts stress the importance of improving vaccination coverage to prevent further spread of the virus.

Men who have sex with men and those with HIV are particularly at risk for severe outcomes.

Efforts are being made to reach at-risk populations and increase access to vaccination.

Public health experts emphasize the need for preventive measures to curb the spread of mpox.
