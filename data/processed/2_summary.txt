
      ‘It’s a new era’: Weight-loss treatments significantly lower blood pressure, studies find
    

New studies show that weight-loss treatments such as the drug tirzepatide and bariatric surgery can significantly reduce blood pressure in adults.

By effectively treating obesity, overall heart health can be improved, leading to benefits beyond just weight loss.

While these interventions show promise, barriers such as high costs and limited insurance coverage may prevent many people from accessing these treatments, potentially worsening health disparities in the US.
