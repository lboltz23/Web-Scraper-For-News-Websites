
      Here’s how long it takes for your body to recover from food poisoning
    

The article discusses the experience of food poisoning, its causes, symptoms, and recovery process.

It explains the different types of bacteria that can cause food poisoning, the time it takes for symptoms to appear, and the importance of staying hydrated during recovery.

The article also provides tips on managing food poisoning symptoms, including staying away from solid foods for 24 hours and gradually reintroducing bland, high-carbohydrate foods.

It also highlights when to seek medical attention for severe cases of food poisoning.
