
      Here’s how long it takes for your body to recover from food poisoning
    

The article discusses the experience of food poisoning, its causes, symptoms, and recovery process.

It explains that the duration of food poisoning varies depending on the type of bacteria ingested and offers tips for recovery, including staying hydrated, avoiding solid foods for 24 hours, and following the BRAT diet.

It also mentions when to seek medical attention for severe symptoms or prolonged illness.

The article emphasizes the importance of proper food safety practices to prevent foodborne illnesses.
