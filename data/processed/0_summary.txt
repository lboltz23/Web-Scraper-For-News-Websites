
      ‘A false sense of security’: Experts say delta-8 THC products can still be dangerous
    

The article discusses the rise in calls to poison centers related to delta-8 products, a cannabis compound marketed as a milder form of THC.

While users may think they are getting a less intense high, the drug can still be dangerous, with adverse effects inclucing hallucinations and dizziness.

Lack of regulation and inconsistent labeling contribute to the risks.

Experts recommend purchasing from reputable sources and avoiding ingestion by children.

Many states have banned or restricted delta-8 products, with efforts to regulate them ongoing.
