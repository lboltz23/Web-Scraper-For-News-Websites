
      ‘A false sense of security’: Experts say delta-8 THC products can still be dangerous
    

The article discusses the rising popularity of delta-8 products, marketed as a milder form of THC but still potentially dangerous.

Calls to poison centers regarding delta-8 exposures spiked in 2022, with concerns about varying potency, inconsistent labeling, and potential adverse effects.

Despite efforts to regulate these products, they remain easily accessible and may pose risks, especially for children.

Experts recommend purchasing from reputable sources and being cautious due to lack of regulations.
