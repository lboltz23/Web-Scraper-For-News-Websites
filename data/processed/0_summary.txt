
      ‘A false sense of security’: Experts say delta-8 THC products can still be dangerous
    

Calls to poison centers about delta-8 products have spiked, with users experiencing adverse effects such as hallucinations and vomiting.

These products, marketed as “diet weed,” are inconsistently labeled and may be appealing to children due to their candy-like appearance.

The lack of regulation and varying potency levels pose potential dangers.

Experts recommend purchasing from reputable sources and being cautious when using these products.
