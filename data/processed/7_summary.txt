
      FDA settles lawsuit over ivermectin content that doctors claimed harmed their practice
    

The FDA settled a lawsuit over social media posts discouraging the use of ivermectin for Covid-19 treatment, causing controversy among conservatives.

The lawsuit, filed by doctors who recommended ivermectin, claimed the FDA interfered with their ability to practice medicine.

The doctors alleged pressure and professional discipline for promoting the drug.

The settlement involves the removal and archiving of disputed FDA materials.

Some see the settlement as a victory for medical freedom.
