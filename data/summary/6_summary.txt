
      Takeaways from the Supreme Court arguments over the abortion drug mifepristone
    

Supreme Court justices expressed skepticism over a potential nationwide ban on mifepristone for medication abortions.

The case revolves around access to the drug and whether the doctors involved had the right to file the lawsuit.

Conservative and liberal justices questioned the necessity of limiting access to mifepristone and the potential impact of a nationwide ban.

The decision is expected by July.

The case was also influenced by judge-shopping tactics.
