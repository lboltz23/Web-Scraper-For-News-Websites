
      ‘A false sense of security’: Experts say delta-8 THC products can still be dangerous
    

The article discusses the increased popularity of delta-8 products, marketed as a milder form of THC.

However, these products can still be dangerous, leading to an increase in calls to poison centers.

The FDA issued warnings about potential risks associated with delta-8, as adverse events, including hospitalizations, have been reported.

With inconsistent labeling and potency, experts recommend buying from reputable sources and storing products safely to prevent accidental ingestion, especially by children.
