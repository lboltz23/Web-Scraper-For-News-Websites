
      ‘A false sense of security’: Experts say delta-8 THC products can still be dangerous
    

The popularity of delta-8 cannabis products, marketed as a milder form of THC, has led to a spike in calls to Poison Centers for help.

Despite the trend leveling off in 2023, concerns remain about the products' potential dangers and inconsistent labeling.

The FDA has issued warnings about delta-8, highlighting adverse effects such as hallucinations and vomiting.

Experts recommend getting cannabis products from reputable sources to ensure safety and accurate dosing.
